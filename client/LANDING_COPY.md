## FAQ

### Is this just a pricing calculator?
No. It is a recommendation engine. The tool looks at use case, team size, seat counts, and current spend to identify where you are overpaying relative to published plan structure.

### Do I need to connect my billing account?
No. The MVP is manual input only. That keeps the tool lightweight and lets users get value without OAuth or procurement friction.

### Why does the report sometimes say I am already spending well?
Because the goal is credibility, not fake savings. If the audit does not find a meaningful downgrade or consolidation opportunity, it should say so clearly.

### What happens if my savings opportunity is large?
If your stack looks like a strong fit for discounted credits, the saved report flow can route you toward a Credex conversation after the audit is shown.

### Does the public share link expose my company or email?
No. Public reports only show the tool mix, savings numbers, and summary. Private lead fields are stored separately from the public share page.

### How do you prevent spam or fake submissions?
The MVP uses a hidden honeypot field and server-side rate limiting on audit creation and lead capture. That keeps the flow fast for humans while blocking the most obvious abuse paths.
